Why combine TFCC recovery and gym reviews?

TFCC injuries often affect real climbing decisions: which problems to avoid, how to manage slab, compression, slopers, mantles, and how to choose gyms with safer training options. This site treats recovery as part of climbing life, not a separate medical topic.

Educational content only. This site does not provide diagnosis or individualized medical treatment.
