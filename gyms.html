Mon–Fri: 12:30–22:30
Sat, Sun & Public Holidays: 11:00–20:00
Contact
Tel: +852 3619 7166
Instagram: @keepclimbinggym
Climbing type
Bouldering

Quick take

Keep Climbing is a small but well-set bouldering gym in Sheung Wan. The space is more compact, so it may feel delicate or less ideal if you are scared of heights, but the routes are very good and the staff are really nice.

Setting style

The setting feels strong, technical, and thoughtful. Based on my sessions, the routes were the highlight of the gym.

Grading

The staff said they followed the japanese style, which is another word for sandbagged. The routes felt high-quality and worth trying.

Atmosphere and community

The staff were really, really nice. This made the gym feel welcoming even though the physical space is relatively compact.

Facilities

  • Rental shoes: Yes
  • Lockers: No
  • Open Cubbies: Yes
  • Changing room: No
  • Washroom: Yes (need keys at the front desk)
  • Shower: No
  • Water / drinks: Bottled watter & other drinks available for a charge
  • Seating area: Yes (little)
  • Training area: Yes
  • Stretching/warm-up area: Yes
  • Hangboard: Yes
  • Kilterboard: No
  • Moonboard: No
  • Tensionboard: No
  • Spray wall: No
  • Air conditioning: Yes
  • Wi-Fi: Yes

Bonus points

  • Really nice staff
  • Convenient Sheung Wan location

Things to know

  • The space is relatively compact
  • If you are scared of heights, this may actually feel more manageable than an average gym in hk
