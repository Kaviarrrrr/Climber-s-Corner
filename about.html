About this project

Built for climbers recovering carefully.

Climber's Corner is a personal website for organizing wrist recovery notes, climbing-gym reviews, and resources that help climbers make more thoughtful training decisions.

Editorial principles

  • Be honest about uncertainty.
  • Separate personal experience from medical information.
  • Do not claim that one exercise fixes every TFCC injury.
  • Review gyms in a specific, practical, and fair way.
  • Prioritize sustainable climbing over short-term ego.

Medical disclaimer

This site is not medical advice. It does not diagnose, treat, or prescribe. Consult a qualified clinician before starting rehabilitation exercises, especially if pain followed a fall, symptoms are severe, or the wrist feels unstable.
